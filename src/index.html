Des Moines Highlights

Here are just some highlights of the many things Des Moines has to offer:

  • Pappajohn Sculpture Park : Open to visitors since September 2009, the Park features artwork by 22 of the world's most celebrated artists.

    The 4.4 Acre park, located within a major crossroads of the urban grid, creates a pedestrian friendly entranceway to downtown Des Moines. This accessible setting, coupled with the skilled landscape design and caliber of the art, makes it unlike any other sculpture park in the United States. The Pappajohns' contribution of 28 works for the park is the most significant donation of artwork ever made to the Des Moines Art Center.

  • East Village : Des Moines's cutting edge, hip neighborhood, with shops, bars, music venues, etc. and the famous Zombie Burger restaurant.
  • Downtown Farmers Market and the River Walk
  • Downtown Court Street area including the Hessen Haus and El Bait Shop:

    The Hessen Haus perfectly recreates the authentic atmosphere of an old-world German bier hall. The Fifty imported German biers on tap, authentic German fare on the menu, and sounds of live music on the stage—including local bands and regional polka—create a lively yet cozy atmosphere where large crowds gather to pass the boot and small groups find themselves making new friends.

    With 185 beers on tap, El Bait Shop has the world’s largest selection of American Craft Beers.

  • Also Adventureland, Blank Park Zoo, State Historical Museum and the beautiful State Capitol and grounds.

On Monday, July 31st we'll be taking an optional all-day trip to take a tour of Okoboji Classic Cars (The Ultimate Man Cave) in northwest Iowa. It is nationally known and an unbelievable place.
